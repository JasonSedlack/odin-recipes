Nog-A-Sake

Description

Nog-A-Sake is from Season 3 Episode 10 of The Office, "A Benihana Christmas". I do not recommend drinking this. "One part eggnog, three parts sake. Some places won't make it for you though because eggnog is seasonal."

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 ounce warm eggnog
  • 1.5 ounce warm Sake

Steps:

  1. Mix into glass and stir.
